The Manufacturing Profit Recovery audit

A 4-6 week, fixed-fee engagement where Stu Jensen comes to your shop, reviews your operation and your financials, and identifies every place you're losing margin. You get a detailed report showing where the money is going, in dollars, and a prioritized action plan for getting it back.

What you get

A complete financial diagnosis

Every margin leak identified and measured in dollars, not percentages. Job costing accuracy, quoting practices, scheduling gaps, overtime patterns, scrap rates, labor utilization, and inventory management.

Root causes, not symptoms

Overtime isn't the problem. It's a symptom of something upstream. The audit traces each leak to its source so the fix actually sticks. You'll see why the money is leaving, not just where.

A prioritized action plan

Fixes ranked by financial impact. The biggest dollars-recovered-per-effort items go first. Most shop owners recover the cost of the audit within the first year.

How the audit works

1

Stu comes to your shop

He works on your floor and in your books, not from a conference room. He works around your production schedule. No all-hands meetings. No workshops. No pulling your people off the line.

2

He finds the leaks

Job costing, quoting, scheduling, overtime, scrap, labor utilization, and inventory management. Anything that affects your margin gets examined. He traces each leak to its root cause and measures it in real dollars.

3

You get the plan

A detailed report showing where the money is going, why it's happening, and what to fix first. The report is yours. You can execute the fixes yourself, or bring Stu back to help.
